In the “Intro,” Hill delves into themes of self-discovery, societal expectations, and personal growth. She reflects on the struggle between public perception and private reality, offering a critique of materialism and superficial values. This sets the stage for the rest of the album, which explores similar themes through a blend of soul, R&B, and hip-hop.
